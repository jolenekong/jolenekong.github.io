After you have taken them home to shower love and affection, it's good to know how to care for them.

Top Ten Tips for Caring For Your Dog

  1. Provide a clean and protected environment for your dog.
  2. Always keep fresh water available.
  3. Feed a quality diet and prevent obesity.
  4. Have your dog examined by a vet on a regular basis.
  5. Provide ample opportunities to exercise.
  6. Communicate with your dog and develop a relationship.
  7. Train your dog to follow simple commands.
  8. Practise reproductive control.
  9. Practise dental care for your dog.
  10. Don't overlook grooming and nail trimming.

Essential tips for caring for your cat

  • Take your pet to the vet for regular checks
  • Outfit your cat with a collar and ID tag
  • Spay or neuter your cat
  • Give your cat a nutritionally balanced diet, including access to fresh water
  • Groom your cat often to keep the coat healthy, soft and shiny
  • Set aside time to play with your cat
